The Borrowers

Book Cover Redesign
Graphic Design II

For this project, we had to choose a book and redesign its cover. I chose "The Borrowers," by Mary Norton, and opted for a cover utilizing photography. In the book, the "Borrowers" are little people that borrow items from humans to use in the home. Therefore, for the objects, I used items mentioned in the story. I then experimented in Photoshop and used stock photos to create my composition. After reaching a concept I liked, I recreated the composition using objects I found in my home.
